About Us

Who Are We?

We are a family of five who have a passion for snow and have inherited the family shop that has been established in 1931 by our great grandfather! Our family business will take care of any needs you need for any snow activities! We are just an email or a phone call away and would be more than happy to provide whatever service you need!

Activites

We host many activities throughout the year and anything that is coming up will be provided on our website with all the information needed! Visit our website to stay updated!

We Are Known Worldwide!

Riders from all over the world stop by our shop and get tune-ups for their gear! Some of the top known professionals come by and ask us for our service! Some of these riders have been with us for many years when they stop by Mt. Hood and ride the great mountain! You can scroll down and see the different riders that have been with us!
