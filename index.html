Renting gear from the Mt. Hood Ski/Snowboarding Shop will be an experience like no other. Not only do we provide the best service in town, but at the lowest costs. Renting from us will save you time and money. The renting process is quick and you won't have to deal with any long rental lines at the ski mountains. We understand that you would rather be out on the slopes enjoying the snow, rather than standing in line and wasting your time.

We are located in Government Camp OR, which is just accross the street from Ski-Bowl, and about 30 mintues from Timberline Lodge and Meadows.

We provide all styles of gear for every age. Whether you're a novice rider who is new to the sport and need information on starting out, or a professional needing a quick tune-up for the shred session, we can and will provide our best service. Check out our website for information on how to get started, or give us a call in the contact section for any questions!
